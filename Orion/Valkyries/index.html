About The Valkyries

Forged in the crucible of Orion arenas, the Valkyries are an advanced team of talented players, representing team work, coordination, & positive sportmanship

Our Mission

To become the best versions of ourselves in and out of the arena. To forge bonds with our teamates, and represent the Valkyries throughout Orion.

Our Values

  • Courage: Facing the void with unwavering resolve.
  • Innovation: Pioneering technology and strategy.
  • Unity: Strength found in collective ambition.
  • Adaptability: Thriving amidst cosmic chaos.
